# Collin County Elections-Roster Reconcile — Research

**Researched:** 2026-07-24
**Domain:** Municipal officeholder-currency verification for 8 Collin County TX cities (17 seats)
whose terms ended/turned over around the May 2, 2026 (and, for Frisco's mayor, June 13, 2026
runoff) general election cycle. DB holds stale 2023-term occupants; this document sources the
AUTHORITATIVE current officeholder + term dates for each seat directly from each city's own
official website plus cross-checked combined-canvass election-result reporting (Community
Impact / city-published results), per the mig-1404 lesson: **never trust a single county's
partial canvass for a multi-county city.**

**Confidence:** HIGH on 14 of 17 seats (direct city-website fetch + combined-canvass election
result, cross-checked from 2+ independent sources). MEDIUM on Plano Place 6 (structural finding
sourced from Wikipedia/Ballotpedia only — plano.gov's JS-rendered platform still blocks
automated fetch, as documented in Phase 220 research). GAP (explicitly flagged, not guessed) on
3 individual email addresses: Murphy Mayor/Place 3/Place 5 (city publishes no council-member
emails at all — confirmed by direct fetch of individual bio pages) and Plano Place 6 (email
unconfirmed — GAP carried over from Phase 220).

**Primary recommendation:** All 17 seats' CURRENT officeholder identity and term math are now
resolved to HIGH or MEDIUM confidence — safe to reconcile into production via idempotent
migrations, following the Phase 219 canvass-citation discipline (cite the combined multi-county
canvass, never a single-county partial). Two structural findings surfaced during this research
that the planner/executor should fold into the reconcile migration's scope, not just the raw
name swaps:

1. **Nevada's DB `valid_to` is almost certainly wrong for Places 3/4/5** — Nevada uses **2-year**
   terms (not 3-year as assumed in Phase 220's spot-check), with Places 3/4/5 elected in
   **odd**-numbered years (last: May 3, 2025) and Mayor/Places 1/2 elected in **even**-numbered
   years (May 2, 2026 cycle — which was itself cancelled this year due to no contest). This means
   the correct `valid_to` for Wilson/Laughter/Little is **2027-05-01**, not `2026-05-01` as Phase
   220's spot-check flagged as merely "expired-but-active." This is a `valid_to` correction, not
   just an officeholder-currency question — recommend folding it into this reconcile migration
   since the research is already done.
2. **Plano "Council Place 6" is not a real vacant council seat — it IS the Mayor's own place
   designation.** Plano's 8-seat council numbers the Mayor as Place 6 (not a separate Place 6
   councilmember alongside the Mayor). The DB's "VACANT Council Place 6" entry is a
   data-modeling artifact if it was created as a distinct office from the Mayor's office; the
   correct fix is either (a) merge/alias Place 6 to the existing Mayor office (John B. Muns), or
   (b) if the DB's schema requires the Mayor and "Place 6" to be modeled as the same
   `politician_id` occupying two `office` rows, populate Place 6 with Muns rather than leaving it
   VACANT. **Do not seat a different, non-existent Place-6-only councilmember** — no such person
   exists.
