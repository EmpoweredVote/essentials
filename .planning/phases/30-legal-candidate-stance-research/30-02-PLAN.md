## Critical Rules

1. **value IS NOT NULL.** Never insert NULL. "Not found" topics get a context-only row.
2. **Description matching, NOT spectrum placement.** McKinney's "law-and-order" framing does NOT auto-map to value 4 or 5 across all topics. Read each topic's literal 1–5 text and place by closest match.
3. **Inference flagging.** When a placement comes from implication, prefix the reasoning with "Inferred from [source/quote]".
4. **Conflict tiebreaker.** Most recent → most specific source wins.
5. **Prohibited sources.** No advocacy scorecards, no social media. Endorsement labels (e.g., LA Forward calling McKinney "carceral") are NOT citations — find the underlying policy quote and cite that.
6. **Paywalled content.** Treat as inaccessible.
