# v16.0 Utah Coverage — Requirements

**Milestone:** v16.0 Utah Coverage
**Status:** Complete (formalized retroactively 2026-06-18)
**Phases:** 139–141
**Migrations:** 777–876 (next: 877)

## Context

Utah was brought to full coverage parity with prior states (CA/OR/ME/MD) in an ad-hoc
push immediately after the v15.0 LA City Stances close. The work was executed and
DB-verified before this milestone record was written; these requirements are reconstructed
from the executed work so the project history stays auditable. Two pillars:

1. **10-city deep-seed** — every Utah city government taken from empty shell to full
   Tier 1 depth (elected roster → headshots → evidence-only compass stances), biggest first:
   Salt Lake City, West Valley City, West Jordan, Provo, Orem, Ogden, Sandy, St. George,
   Lehi, Layton.
2. **Full state legislature stances** — evidence-only compass stances for every one of the
   104 Utah state legislators (29 Senate + 75 House), one research agent at a time.

Utah TIGER geofences and the legislature roster/chambers pre-existed this milestone.
