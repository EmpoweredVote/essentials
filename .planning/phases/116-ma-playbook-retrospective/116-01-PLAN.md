**GOTCHA 1 — Insert in Step 3 (Geofence Sources), after the last existing [GOTCHA] in that step:**

Title: Massachusetts requires BOTH G4110 cities and G4040 COUSUB towns — G4110-only leaves the majority of MA addresses unrouted

Content: Massachusetts has 58 incorporated G4110 cities loaded in v5.0, but the majority of MA residential addresses fall in G4040 COUSUB towns (293 rows loaded v5.0). Loading only the G4110 city layer means residents in Concord, Lexington, Brookline, and the other 290 towns receive no LOCAL district routing. This is a Massachusetts outlier — unlike states like Texas or California where most residents live in G4110 incorporated places. For any MA onboarding: assert both layers present before seeding any city. Zero-row gate: `SELECT COUNT(*) FROM essentials.geofence_boundaries WHERE state='25' AND mtfcc='G4040'` must return 293. Do NOT re-run the COUSUB loader — it was loaded in v5.0 (ON CONFLICT DO NOTHING gives false sense of re-load; assertion is the correct pattern). Cambridge (FUNCSTAT=F) and Boston (FUNCSTAT=I) are correctly absent from the G4040 layer — they appear only in G4110. Phase 107 confirmed.
