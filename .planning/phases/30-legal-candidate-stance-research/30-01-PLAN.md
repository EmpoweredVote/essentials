## Critical Rules

1. **value IS NOT NULL** — never insert a NULL value. "Not found" topics get a context-only row.
2. **Description matching, NOT spectrum placement.** Read the 1–5 stance text for each topic and pick the description Ashouri's stated position best matches. Value 3 is a defined position, not a "moderate."
3. **Inference flagging.** When placement comes from implication rather than a direct quote, prefix the reasoning narrative with "Inferred from [source/quote]" so future researchers see the leap.
4. **Conflict tiebreaker.** Most recent source wins; equally recent → most specific source wins (detailed Q&A > vague campaign blurb).
5. **Prohibited sources.** Never base a value on third-party advocacy scorecards (ACLU/NAACP grades) or social media (Twitter/X, Facebook). Endorsements may point you to where to look but are not citations themselves.
6. **Paywalled content.** Treat as inaccessible — equivalent to "not found" for that source.
