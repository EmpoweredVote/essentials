**Research date:** 2026-05-03
**Researcher:** gsd-phase-researcher
**Sources consulted:** LWV Collin County voter guides (PDFs), Community Impact Q&A articles, candidate campaign websites, news coverage

---

## SCOPE NOTES

The LWV Collin County voter guides ask about **Housing** and **Finance/Affordability** but do NOT ask about data centers, climate, civil rights, immigration, reproductive rights, or other compass topics. As a result, most stance assignments below are for Housing (topic) and Taxes (topic) only. No other topics had findable documented positions for city council members in this county.

The LWV questions are:
- **Housing / Land Use:** "What is your vision for housing and land use in the city?"
- **Affordability / Finance:** "What changes, if any, are needed to make the city more affordable?"

These map imperfectly to the 5-point compass scale. Assignments are MEDIUM confidence unless the language is extremely specific.
