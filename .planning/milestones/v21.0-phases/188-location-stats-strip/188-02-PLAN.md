<objective>
Add the pure resolver that maps a banner's location identity to a population number (or null on any
miss). `resolvePopulation({ tier, geoId, city, stateAbbrev })` implements D-04 (state abbrev -> FIPS),
D-05 (city via geo_id OR name|state index), D-06 (federal US total), and D-07/STAT-03 (null-on-miss).
It imports the Plan 01 bundle and inverts the now-exported `STATE_FIPS_TO_ABBREV` as the single source
of truth for abbrev -> FIPS. Ship it with a fixture-injected Vitest matrix following the repo's
pure-logic (no jsdom) convention.

Purpose: The resolution half of STAT-02 and the mechanical guarantee of STAT-03. Keeping resolution a
pure function (no I/O, no router) is what lets both parents (Plan 03) call it identically and keeps
SectionBanner presentational — directly serving Phase 189's single-shared-component goal.
Output: `src/lib/population.js`, `src/lib/population.test.js`.
</objective>
