Build the PowerShell generator `generate_md_senate.ps1` and produce migration `273_md_state_senators.sql` to seed 47 Maryland state senators with offices linked to STATE_UPPER SLDU districts (geo_ids 24001..24047). Senator roster verified from mgaleg.maryland.gov, 2026-06-02. No vacant Senate seats.

Purpose: Implements MD-GOV-03. Follows the OR senator generator pattern with three critical MD-specific deviations: (1) FIPS prefix `24` and PadLeft(3) geo_id format, (2) `AND state = 'MD'` in government subquery, and (3) `p.id IS NOT NULL` guard added to defend against silent CROSS JOIN failure on DO NOTHING conflict. Migration depends on chamber 'Maryland Senate' existing (Plan 93-01).

Output: 2 files — generator .ps1 and generated .sql. SQL migration applied to production via mcp__supabase-local__apply_migration. 47 politicians + 47 offices + 47 office_id back-fills.
</objective>
