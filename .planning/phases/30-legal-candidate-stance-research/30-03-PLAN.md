3. **Inference flagging.** Prefix reasoning with "Inferred from [source/quote]" when placement comes from implication.
4. **Conflict tiebreaker.** Most recent → most specific.
5. **Prohibited sources.** No advocacy scorecards, no social media. LA Forward's *endorsement* of Roy is not a citation; the policy positions LA Forward describes (and Roy stated) are.
6. **Paywalled content.** Treat as inaccessible.
