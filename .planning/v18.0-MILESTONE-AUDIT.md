## Known Issues / Follow-up (D-08)

All four D-08 items are recorded here. Items 1–2 are deferred-by-design (not broken). Items 3–4
are carry-forward notes.

### 1. NV Legislature Compass Stances (D-08.1) — deferred to dedicated follow-up

All 63 NV legislators (21 Senate + 42 Assembly) are seeded with offices linked to SLDU/SLDL
geofences and 63/63 headshots at 600×750. **Compass stances were explicitly deferred** in the v18.0
scope (ROADMAP.md convention: "Legislature compass stances are deferred to a follow-up milestone").
This follows the Oregon pattern: v8.0 (OR) seeded legislators + headshots only; v9.0 (OR) delivered
536 evidence-only stance rows for all 90 OR legislators. A Nevada equivalent — working title "v18.1
NV Legislature Stances" — is the natural follow-up. Not a close blocker.

### 2. Mesquite (D-08.2) — future Clark County wave

Mesquite (Clark County's smallest incorporated city, pop. ~20,000) was not seeded in v18.0.
It is a candidate for a future Clark County wave alongside any other unincorporated city structures
that warrant dedicated coverage. Not a close blocker.

### 3. Browse-Government-List State-Leak Bug (D-08.3) — deferred fix, not blocking

Confirmed during Phase 163 (Henderson): browsing an **unseeded city** via the government-list
browse endpoint leaks stale prior-location officials under the wrong state banner. Example:
navigating to an unseeded NV city shows a residual prior-location government section rather than
an empty state. Tracked in memory as `project_browse_government_list_state_leak`. The root cause
is in the browse endpoint's state-filtering logic when no matching government rows exist.
This is a backend follow-up fix, not a v18.0 data issue. Fix deferred; not blocking close.

### 4. Phase Renumber 169→173 (D-08.4) — context note for future readers

Phase numbers **169–172** are occupied by the **parked v19.0 frontend detour** (dark-mode design
system, section banners, banner asset pipeline, elections-page parity) whose completed directories
live at `.planning/phases/169…172-*`. The v18.0 Nevada Playbook Retrospective was originally
numbered 169 but was renumbered to **173** on 2026-06-30 to avoid the directory collision with the
v19.0 detour phases. Numeric execution order is otherwise unaffected (158 → … → 168 → 173).
ROADMAP.md, STATE.md, and REQUIREMENTS.md were updated + committed (`5498e9e`) before Phase 173
execution began.
