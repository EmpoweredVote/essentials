## Integration Assessment

### Phase 32 Gap Closure Verified

Both critical gaps from the 2026-05-09 audit are closed:

**Gap 1 — COMPASS-05 (City Attorney candidate judicial compass):** Fixed in CandidateProfile.jsx line 198. The `isLegalCandidate ? 'judicial' : null` fallback ensures city attorneys with null `district_type` route to JudicialCompassSection. Ashouri, McKinney, and Roy stances (Phases 27, 30) are now reachable via the UI.

**Gap 2 — DONOR-04 (Incumbent judge Profile.jsx):** Fixed in Profile.jsx. LegalDonorActivitySection imported and rendered behind `{isLegalPolitician && ...}` gate (line 239). Connolly, Draper, Walgren donor firm data is now visible on `/politician/:id`.

**isLegalCandidate parity:** Profile.jsx now has 6 conditions (JUDICIAL, NATIONAL_JUDICIAL, city attorney, district attorney, judge, justice), matching the base parity set. CandidateProfile.jsx has 8 conditions (same 6 + `position_name` fallbacks using candidate data). This asymmetry is intentional — incumbents have reliable `office_title`; candidates use `position_name` as an additional signal.

### Backend Route Ordering

`/:id/legal-donor-activity` (line 331) is registered before the `/:id` catch-all (line 355) in essentialsPoliticians.ts. No Express shadowing risk.

### Narrow Future-Path Gap (Tech Debt, Not Blocking)

Profile.jsx `districtScope` derivation (lines 209-216) has no `isLegalCandidate` fallback. This means a city attorney/DA incumbent politician record with `district_type ≠ 'JUDICIAL'` would get CompassCard instead of JudicialCompassSection. **No current DB records trigger this path** — all incumbent judge records have `district_type=JUDICIAL`, and the current City Attorney race candidates (Ashouri, McKinney, Roy) are accessed via CandidateProfile.jsx. Track for future data entry.
